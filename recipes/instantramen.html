Ingredients

  • instant noodle from walmart.

Steps

  1. Remove the noodles from the package.
  2. Put the noodles into a microwave safe bowl and cover with water.
  3. Microwave your ramen for between three and five minutes.
  4. Let the noodles sit for about three minutes after the microwave stops.
  5. Mix in the flavor packet.
