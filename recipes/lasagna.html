Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • 1 lb. noodles
  • salt
  • pepper
  • one large egg
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 (28-oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Put pasta in boiling water and cook until done
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef with bread crumbs, parsley, Parmesan, egg, garlic, 1 teaspoon salt, and red pepper flakes. Mix until just combined then form into 16 balls.
  3. Get out large pot and put oil into medium heat.
  4. Add meatballs to pot and cook until browned on all sides. Add to plate.
  5. Add onion to pot and cook until soft
  6. Add crushed tomatoes and bayleaf
  7. Add salt and pepper
  8. cook until a simmer
  9. add meatballs back into pot and cover
  10. simmer for 8-10 minutes
  11. serve pasta with sauce and parmesan
