Spaghetti Recipe

Disclaimer: I am too lazy to make lasagna, but I am all in for making Spaghetti.

Ingredients

    1 lb. ground beef
    tomato sauce
    1 lb. noodles
    salt
    pepper
    one large egg
    2 garlic cloves, minced
    1 bay leaf
    1 (28-oz.) can crushed tomatoes
    1/2 onion, finely chopped
    2 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
